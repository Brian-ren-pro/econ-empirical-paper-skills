# Finance Review Rubric

Score each dimension from 0 to 100.

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | What To Evaluate |
|---|---:|---|
| Contribution | 20 | Novelty, importance, field fit |
| Identification | 25 | Shock/design credibility, confounds, causal language |
| Empirical Execution | 20 | Data, variables, specifications, clustering, tables |
| Mechanism Discipline | 15 | Mechanism evidence vs overclaiming |
| Literature Positioning | 10 | Core references, framing, subfield language |
| Writing and Structure | 10 | Organization, clarity, table order, reader guidance |

## Score Bands

- 90-100: unusually strong; near submission-ready.
- 80-89: strong; likely minor-to-moderate revision.
- 70-79: promising but needs major revision pressure.
- 60-69: serious concerns; major redesign likely.
- Below 60: reject-like unless a clear rescue path exists.

## Common Finance Reviewer Red Flags

- Treatment is institutional but text claims a narrow mechanism.
- Robustness tables are used as if they prove mechanism.
- Heterogeneity is described as causal mechanism.
- Main result and downstream outcome are treated as equally identified.
- Table numbering or source mapping is unclear.
- Modern DID checks are either ignored or over-positioned.
