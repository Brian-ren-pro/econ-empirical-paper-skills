# Claim-Evidence Matrix

Use this template to keep manuscript claims tied to evidence.

## Template

| Claim | Location | Evidence | Evidence Type | Strength | Allowed Wording | Unresolved Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Short claim | Ch./section | Table/Figure/Lit | main / support / mechanism / diagnostic | high / medium / low | exact language | what remains uncertain |

## Strength Guide

- `high`: central table/figure directly supports the claim under the main design.
- `medium`: pattern is consistent but not decisive alone.
- `low`: suggestive, contextual, or dependent on strong interpretation.

## Allowed Wording

Match wording to evidence strength:

- High: "we find", "the results show", "the estimates indicate".
- Medium: "the pattern is consistent with", "supports the interpretation that".
- Low: "suggests", "is compatible with", "points toward".

## Red Flags

- Claim says "mechanism" when evidence is only heterogeneity.
- Claim says "coordination" when only contract tightening is shown.
- Claim says "rules out" when evidence only weakens an alternative.
- Claim cites a supporting diagnostic as if it were the main result.
