# Stage Routing

## Stage 1: Idea / Early Materials

Signals:

- research idea only;
- notes or literature fragments;
- preliminary tables;
- no complete empirical result package.

Route:

- `econ-empirical-paper`
- mode: architecture

Exit condition:

- paper architecture and material gaps.

## Stage 2: Partial Draft

Signals:

- some chapters exist;
- advisor memo or proposal exists;
- no complete manuscript.

Route:

- `econ-empirical-paper`
- mode: chapter-writing or full-text-integration.

Exit condition:

- complete draft.

## Stage 3: Complete Draft / Missing Explicit Pre-Review Package

Signals:

- full manuscript exists;
- tables or figures are embedded or generated;
- source mapping, result summary, or unresolved issues list is missing.

Route:

- `econ-empirical-paper`
- mode: pre-review-packaging.

Exit condition:

- complete draft plus pre-review package.

## Stage 4: Complete Draft With Pre-Review Package

Signals:

- complete manuscript;
- exhibit map;
- table/figure role map;
- result summary;
- literature/mechanism summary;
- unresolved issues list.

Route:

- `econ-empirical-paper-reviewer`
- mode: full review.

Exit condition:

- reviewer reports, EIC decision, revision roadmap.

## Stage 5: Reviewed Draft

Signals:

- reviewer panel reports;
- external reviewer comments;
- EIC decision or revision roadmap.

Route:

- `econ-empirical-paper`
- mode: revision.

Exit condition:

- revised draft and revision delta log.

## Stage 6: Revised Draft

Signals:

- revised manuscript exists;
- response or delta log exists.

Route:

- optional future reviewer re-review;
- or ready for human review.
